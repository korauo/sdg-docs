# 9. Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure
<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-09-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

##**Objective**

**SDG 9 aims to build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation.**

## Targets:
    
### 9.1. Develop quality, reliable, sustainable, and resilient infrastructure.

### 9.2. Promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization.

### 9.3. Increase access to financial services and markets for small-scale industrial and other enterprises.
