# 14. Life Below Water
<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-14-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

##**Objective**

**SDG 14 seeks to conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources**

## Targets:
    
### 14.1. Prevent and significantly reduce marine pollution of all kinds.

### 14.2. Sustainably manage and protect marine and coastal ecosystems.

### 14.3. Minimize and address the impacts of ocean acidification.
