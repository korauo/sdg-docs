# 10. Reduced Inequalities
<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-10-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

##**Objective**

**SDG 10 is committed to reducing inequality within and among countries.**

## Targets:
    
### 10.1. Progressively achieve and sustain income growth of the bottom 40 percent of the population.

### 10.2. Empower and promote the social, economic, and political inclusion of all, irrespective of age, sex, disability, race, ethnicity, origin, religion, or economic or other statuses.

### 10.3. Ensure equal opportunity and reduce inequalities of outcome.
