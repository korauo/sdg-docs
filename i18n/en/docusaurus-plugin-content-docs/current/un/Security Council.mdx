# Security Council (SC)

<p align="center"><img src="https://www.mun.bme.hu/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Security-Council.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

##**Objective**

**The Security Council is one of the six main organs of the UN. Its primary responsibility is to maintain international peace and security.**

## Targets:
    
### SC.1 Determine the existence of a threat to peace or act of aggression and recommend strategies for its resolution.

### SC.2 Establish peacekeeping operations and international sanctions to restore international peace and security.

### UN.3 Foster global development and cooperation to ensure sustainable living standards for all through the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and other developmental agendas.
