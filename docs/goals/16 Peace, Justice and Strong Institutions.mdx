# 16. Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions
<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-16-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

##**Objective**

**SDG 16 aims to promote peaceful and inclusive societies, provide access to justice for all, and build effective, accountable, and inclusive institutions.**

## Targets:
    
### 16.1. Reduce all forms of violence and related death rates everywhere

### 16.2. End abuse, exploitation, trafficking, and all forms of violence and torture against children.

### 16.3. Promote the rule of law at the national and international levels.
