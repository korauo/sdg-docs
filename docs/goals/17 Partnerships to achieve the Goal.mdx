# 17. Partnerships for the goals
<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-17-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

##**Objective**

**SDG 17 seeks to strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development.**

## Targets:
    
### 17.1. Strengthen domestic resource mobilization to improve domestic capacity for tax and other revenue collection.

### 17.2. Implement and operationalize a global strategy for development and operationalize effective partnerships.

### 17.3. Enhance international support for implementing effective and targeted capacity-building in developing countries.
