# United Nations (UN)

<p align="center"><img src="	https://www.un.org/sites/un2.un.org/files/un-emblem.svg" width="200" height="200"/></p>

##**Objective**

**The United Nations is an international organization founded in 1945, committed to maintaining international peace and security, promoting human rights, fostering social and economic development, protecting the environment, and providing humanitarian aid in cases of famine, natural disaster, and armed conflict**

## Targets:
    
### UN.1 Promote and establish international peace and security through conflict prevention, peacekeeping, and conflict resolution.

### UN.2 Promote and protect human rights through instruments like the Universal Declaration of Human Rights and mechanisms like the Human Rights Council.

### UN.3 Foster global development and cooperation to ensure sustainable living standards for all through the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and other developmental agendas.
