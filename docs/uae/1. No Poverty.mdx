# Goal 1: No Poverty

<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-01-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

The UAE is committed to eradicating poverty and enhancing the quality of life for all its residents. Through various social welfare programs and economic initiatives, the UAE aims to reduce poverty and provide economic security.

## Key Initiatives

-**Social Welfare Programs**: The UAE offers various financial aid programs for low-income families and people with special needs.
-**Job Creation Programs**: The UAE supports job creation initiatives to provide stable income opportunities for all.
