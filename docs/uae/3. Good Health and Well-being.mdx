# Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being

<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-03-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

Ensuring the health and well-being of all residents is a top priority in the UAE, demonstrated by its investments in healthcare infrastructure.

## Key Initiatives

-**Quality Healthcare Access**: The UAE offers advanced healthcare services and hospitals accessible to all citizens and residents.
-**Health Awareness Programs**: Initiatives like the National Immunization Program aim to promote health education.
