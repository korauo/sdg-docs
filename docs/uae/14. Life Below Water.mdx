# Goal 14: Life Below Water

<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-14-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

The UAE is actively working to protect marine environments and promote sustainable use of ocean resources.

## Key Initiatives

-**Marine Conservation Efforts**: Programs to protect coral reefs, marine wildlife, and coastal ecosystems.
-**Sustainable Fisheries**: Regulations to ensure sustainable fishing practices and prevent overfishing.
