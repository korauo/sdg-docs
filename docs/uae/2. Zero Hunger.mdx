# Goal 2: Zero Hunger

<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-02-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

The UAE has adopted several strategies to improve food security and promote sustainable agriculture, despite its arid climate.

## Key Initiatives

-**National Food Security Strategy**: Focuses on enhancing food supply chains and sustainable agricultural practices.
-**Food Security Initiatives**: Includes programs to reduce food waste and increase local food production.

These efforts align with global efforts to eradicate hunger and ensure food availability.
