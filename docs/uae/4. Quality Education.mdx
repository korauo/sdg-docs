# Goal 4: Quality Education

<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-04-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

The UAE prioritizes education as a core part of its national vision, aiming to ensure that all citizens have access to high-quality education.

## Key Initiatives

-**UAE Vision 2021**: Focuses on providing high-quality, inclusive education for all.
-**Innovation in Education**: Programs promoting STEM and digital skills development among youth.
