# Goal 5: Gender Equality

<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-05-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

The UAE has made substantial progress in empowering women and promoting gender equality in various sectors.

## Key Initiatives

-**Emirati Women's Day**: Celebrates the achievements of Emirati women and promotes gender equality.
-**Equal Opportunity Policies**: Ensures equal pay and opportunities for women in the workforce.
